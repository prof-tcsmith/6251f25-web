1 Assignment Overview

1.1 Business Context

You are a data analyst at PrimeRealty Analytics, a consulting firm that helps real estate companies and investors make data-driven decisions. Your client, a large real estate investment firm, wants to develop a pricing model to:

  1. Accurately predict property values for investment decisions
  2. Identify which features most strongly influence property prices
  3. Understand if their current pricing strategies are optimal

The firm has provided you with data on recent property listings including property characteristics, neighborhood information, and market conditions.

Assignment Objectives
  1. Build and evaluate linear regression models for price prediction
  2. Perform feature engineering to improve model performance
  3. Interpret model coefficients in business context
  4. Diagnose and address model assumptions
  5. Provide actionable insights for investment strategy

1.2 Dataset Description

The data consists of two CSV files:

  • housing_properties.csv: Property listings with features and prices
  • economic_indicators.csv: Monthly economic indicators affecting the market

9 Tips for Success

Best Practices
  1. Start with simple models: Build complexity gradually
  2. Validate assumptions: Linear regression has specific requirements
  3. Consider business logic: Do the relationships make sense?
  4. Document decisions: Explain your choices
  5. Think like a consultant: What would the client want to know?
