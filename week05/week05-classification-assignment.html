1 Assignment Overview

1.1 Business Context

You are a data scientist at HealthAnalytics Pro, a consulting firm specializing in healthcare analytics. Your client, a large hospital network, wants to reduce their 30-day readmission rates to:

  1. Improve patient outcomes and quality of care
  2. Avoid Medicare penalties for excessive readmissions
  3. Optimize resource allocation and discharge planning
  4. Identify high-risk patients for targeted interventions

The hospital has provided you with historical patient data including demographics, clinical information, and readmission outcomes.

Assignment Objectives
  1. Build and evaluate classification models to predict 30-day readmissions
  2. Handle class imbalance appropriately
  3. Optimize for the right performance metrics given the business context
  4. Identify key risk factors for readmission
  5. Provide actionable recommendations for intervention programs

1.2 Dataset Description

The data consists of three CSV files:

  • patient_readmissions.csv: Patient admissions with features and readmission outcomes
  • patient_lab_results.csv: Laboratory test results for each patient
  • patient_medications.csv: Medications prescribed to each patient

9 Tips for Success

Best Practices
  1. Consider the business context: False negatives (missing high-risk patients) may be more costly than false positives
  2. Handle imbalance carefully: Don’t just optimize for accuracy
  3. Feature engineering matters: Domain knowledge can significantly improve performance
  4. Interpret results: Healthcare professionals need to understand why a patient is flagged
  5. Think about deployment: How would this model be used in practice?
