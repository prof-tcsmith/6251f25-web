1 Assignment Overview

1.1 Business Context

You are a data scientist at RetailGenius Analytics, a company that helps e-commerce businesses optimize their customer experience and marketing strategies. Your client, a large online retailer, wants to:

  1. Segment customers for targeted marketing campaigns
  2. Build a recommendation system to increase sales
  3. Identify similar customers for lookalike audiences
  4. Optimize product recommendations based on customer behavior

The client has provided you with customer behavior data, product catalog, and purchase history.

Assignment Objectives
  1. Implement KNN for customer segmentation and similarity analysis
  2. Understand the impact of distance metrics and k values
  3. Build a product recommendation system using KNN
  4. Apply proper feature scaling and selection techniques
  5. Evaluate model performance using appropriate metrics

1.2 Dataset Description

The data consists of three CSV files:

  • customer_behavior.csv: Customer demographics and behavioral metrics
  • product_catalog.csv: Product information and attributes
  • purchase_history.csv: Transaction records for collaborative filtering

9 Tips for Success

Best Practices
  1. Feature Selection: Not all features are equally important for similarity
  2. Scaling is Critical: KNN is sensitive to feature scales
  3. Curse of Dimensionality: Consider PCA if you have many features
  4. Business Context: Ensure segments and recommendations make business sense
  5. Computational Efficiency: KNN can be slow with large datasets - consider optimization
